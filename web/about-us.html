About Us

The Team

Meet the highly trained engineering trio from POLYTECH High School. We are seniors in the Engineering Design Technology (EDT) career pathway, taught by the legendary Mr. Robert Bogdziewicz. Unlike other schools, POLYTECH provides more than 9 credits for engineering courses, 6 of which are nationally recognized Project Lead The Way (PLTW) courses. Our current class, Engineering Design and Development (EDD) is one such PLTW course. It is a project-oriented engineering capstone course aimed at teaching students to work cooperatively to solve technical problems.
